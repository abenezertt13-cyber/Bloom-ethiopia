Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas in Addis Ababa do you deliver to?
We deliver to all 11 sub-cities of Addis Ababa including Bole, Arada (Arat Kilo/Piassa), Kirkos (Kazanchis), Yeka (Megenagna/Ayat), Nifas Silk (Sarbet/Summit), CMC, Kolfe, Lideta, Akaky Kaliti, and more. Same-day delivery is free for all Addis Ababa orders.
How do I pay with Telebirr or Chapa?
After you submit your order, we will call you to confirm availability and total price. Then we will send you a payment request via Telebirr or provide a Chapa payment link. You can also choose Cash on Delivery and pay when the flowers arrive.
Can I send flowers to someone in another city like Hawassa or Dire Dawa?
Yes! We deliver to Dire Dawa, Hawassa, Adama (Nazret), Bahir Dar, Mekelle, Jimma, and Dessie with next-day delivery. For other towns, please call us at 0966 211 851 and we will arrange special logistics.
What if I need flowers for a funeral urgently?
Funeral and memorial orders are our highest priority. We accept urgent orders outside regular hours for these occasions. Call 0966 211 851 directly - we answer funeral inquiries 24/7 and prioritize respectful, timely delivery.
Do you offer flowers for Ethiopian holidays like Enkutatash and Meskel?
Absolutely! We create special arrangements for Enkutatash (Ethiopian New Year), Meskel (Demera), Timkat, and Fasika (Easter). These are our busiest seasons, so we recommend pre-ordering at least 3-5 days in advance to guarantee availability.
Can I customize my bouquet?
Yes! Use the "Flower Preference" field in the order form to describe what you want, or call/WhatsApp us at 0966 211 851. We can customize colors, flower types, sizes, and add extras like chocolates, balloons, or teddy bears.
